Our story

Hey, I'm Bharath(to your left). I started aaraike nursery with my friend Manu. At first we used to grow saplings from scratch. Soon we felt it's quiet a long process and searched for a shortcut. Luckily we got a wholesale suppliers of tiny little plant saplings. We bought those and nurtured them until they reach planting stage, then started to supply them for local farmers.

Our plants(I'm not bragging) have a success rate of 96% which is extremely rare. Our farmers are totally happy about the yield they got and promoted us everywhere. We have almost 50% of grape farms in Chikkaballapur district outskirts and few parts of Andhra too.

We also built 80+ grapefarms of new farmers from scratch and helping them to manage. We believe grape farm is one of the best investments anybody can make. Coz of it's high ROI.
